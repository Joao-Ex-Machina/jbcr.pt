Gallery

Welcome to my analog photography gallery!

Most of the photos here are taken with a Ricoh KR-5 and are normally developed at the awesome Carmencita Lisboa film lab and scanned in the NAF lab. Sometimes I also develop on my own, with various results.

As of now my favorite stocks to shoot are SantaColor 100, Kodak ColorPlus 200 and Kentmere Pan 400. I really liked shooting Portra too.
